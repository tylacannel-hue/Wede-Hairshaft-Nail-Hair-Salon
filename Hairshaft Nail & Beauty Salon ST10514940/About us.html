About Us

Our Story


The Hairshaft nail & beauty salon was inspired by my love of doing hair and making the ladies in my family feel beautiful. The idea sparked many years ago when my sister was getting married and she had asked me to do her hair as well as her bridesmaids hair, it was such an honour to do the hair and with the results I had given them, they all encouraged me start a business where clients book me and I travel to whichever destination they choose. This experience was over six month when I then decided it was time to expand my business where by I provide additional services for my clients. A few months after the expansion I found a cute store to rent and begin a new chapter for my business. The goal I have accomplished by building this business, has truly been a blessing. The aim for the hair salon is to ensure that all my clients and potential clients have the best of the best and walk away with an experience they will nevr forget.
